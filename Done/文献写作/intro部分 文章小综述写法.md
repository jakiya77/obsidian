For  example, the **spatial diversity gain** of a single MA/FAS  over a 1D line was revealed in [25] and [26] based on a  simplified spatial-correlation channel model. 

Then, using  a general field-response channel model, the advantage of  an MA for **increasing** **the received signal power** over a  2D surface was explored in [11] in the scenarios with  either a finite or infinite number of channel paths, which was  further extended to the MA-aided wideband systems over a 3D  space [27] and MA-aided multiple-input single-output (MISO)  systems [28]. 

Moreover, the **spatial multiplexing performance**  of MA-aided MIMO systems was characterized in [12],  [29], and [30]. 

The MA-assisted multiuser communication  has also been widely investigated [13], [31], [32], [33],  [34], [35], [36], [37], [38], where the **multiuser interference  can be efficiently mitigated** by the joint optimization of  MAs’ positions and precoder/combiner.
