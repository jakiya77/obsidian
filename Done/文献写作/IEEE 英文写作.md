### 一、 学术英语写作的“四大核心心法”

#### 1. 客观与正式 (Objective & Formal)

学术论文不是散文，也不是聊天。你需要剔除所有的主观情绪和口语表达。

- **绝对禁止缩写：** `don't` 改为 `do not`，`can't` 改为 `cannot`，`it's` 改为 `it is`。
    
- **戒除口语词汇：** 像 `get`, `a lot of`, `huge`, `good`, `bad`, `fix` 这些词，在学术写作中几乎是“违禁词”（后文有替换表）。
    
- **慎用第一人称：** 虽然现在的 IEEE 论文允许使用 `We`（例如 _We propose..._），但尽量多混合使用**被动语态**或**客观物作主语**（例如 _The proposed algorithm achieves..._ 或者 _It is observed that..._）。
    

#### 2. 精准与简洁 (Precise & Concise)

审稿人通常都很忙，不要让他们猜你的意思。**One sentence, one idea (一句话只表达一个意思)。**

- **避免过度嵌套：** 不要写那种带着三四个 `which` 和 `that` 的超长从句。如果句子超过三行，果断把它拆成两句。
    
- **使用形式主语：** 避免头重脚轻。不要写 _Solving the highly non-convex and coupled problem is difficult._，要写成 _It is intractable to solve the highly non-convex..._
    

#### 3. 逻辑连贯 (Cohesive)

英文写作是“形合”语言，必须通过显性的逻辑连接词来引导读者。

- **递进/补充：** Furthermore, Moreover, In addition, Additionally.
    
- **转折/对比：** However, Nevertheless, On the contrary, In contrast.
    
- **因果/结论：** Consequently, Therefore, Thus, As a result, Hence.
    
- **举例/解释：** Specifically, Namely, For instance, In particular.
    

#### 4. 坚定但严谨 (Assertive but Cautious)

学术界讲究“留有余地”。不要把话说死，除非你在做纯数学的绝对证明。

- **尽量不用：** `prove` (证明，太绝对), `guarantee` (保证), `absolutely`, `always`.
    
- **推荐使用：** `suggest` (表明), `indicate` (显示), `demonstrate` (证实/展示), `reveal` (揭示), `can/may` (可能)。

### 三、 IEEE 论文各部分的“八股文”句型

学术写作就像搭积木，掌握了经典句式，往里面填变量就可以了。

#### 1. 引言部分 (Introduction)

- **交代背景：** In recent years, [研究方向] has drawn significant attention due to its potential in...
    
- **指出缺陷 (However是灵魂)：** Despite the fruitful results, most existing works assume... However, in practical scenarios, this assumption is often invalid.
    
- **引出本文：** Motivated by the aforementioned challenges, this paper investigates...
    
- **总结贡献：** The main contributions of this paper are summarized as follows: Firstly, we propose... Secondly, we formulate...
    

#### 2. 系统模型与问题描述 (System Model & Formulation)

- **定义变量：** Let $x$ denote the... / We define $y$ as...
    
- **描述公式：** The channel matrix can be modeled as... / The objective function is formulated as...
    
- **提出难点：** It is worth noting that Problem (13) is highly non-convex owing to the coupled variables...
    

#### 3. 算法与优化 (Algorithm Design)

- **总体策略：** To tackle the intractability, we propose an alternating optimization (AO) framework...
    
- **具体步骤：** Specifically, the original problem is decomposed into two subproblems...
    
- **收敛性/复杂度：** The proposed algorithm is guaranteed to converge to a stationary point... The overall computational complexity is $\mathcal{O}(...)$.
    

#### 4. 仿真结果 (Simulation Results)

- **描述图表：** As illustrated in Fig. X, the achievable secrecy rate is plotted versus...
    
- **对比优势：** It can be clearly observed that the proposed MFDA scheme consistently outperforms the baseline FDA scheme.
    
- **解释原因：** This phenomenon can be fundamentally attributed to the fact that...
    

#### 5. 结论 (Conclusion)

- **重申所做工作：** In this letter, we have investigated the secure transmission problem in...
    
- **重申所提方法：** We have proposed a novel 2D-MFDA architecture and developed a BSUM-based optimization algorithm.
    
- **一句话亮点：** Extensive simulation results have demonstrated the superiority and robustness of the proposed design under various stringent constraints.

### 💡 常见的“中式英语”避坑提醒

1. **“There is/are” 滥用：**
    
    - _❌ 错误:_ There are many researchers studying this problem.
        
    - _✅ 正确:_ Numerous researchers _have investigated_ this problem.
        
2. **拟人化主语不当：**
    
    - _❌ 错误:_ The paper thinks MFDA is better. (论文不会思考)
        
    - _✅ 正确:_ This paper _argues/demonstrates_ that MFDA achieves superior performance.
        
3. **介词搭配生硬：** 多查字典，比如“对……的影响”是 `impact on` 而不是 `impact to`；“以……为前提”是 `subject to`。
